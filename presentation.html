User Journey

From Worry to Wake-Up in 4 Steps

1

Open Friends Dashboard

See who's sleeping, active, or snoozing. The Security Circle flags any location anomalies instantly.

2

Select a Friend → Configure Wake-Up

Choose time, method, soundscape, duration, and write a note — or let Solaris AI craft the perfect message.

3

Schedule → Nudge Log goes live

A bypass-DND push notification fires. Real-time log shows every ping dispatched. Live countdown tracks engagement.

4

Proof of Life Alarm fires on the recipient's phone

Front camera opens. They must take a selfie — or answer 8×7 — to dismiss. No ghosting allowed.
